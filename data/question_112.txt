Q: Can I travel while on OPT? A: If you are on pre-completion OPT, review our [travel instructions for current students](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/current-students/travel-and-visitors.html). If you are on post-completion or STEM OPT, review our [travel instructions for alumni](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/alumni/travel/documents-to-carry.html).
