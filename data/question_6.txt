Q: Can I still apply for my I-20/DS-2019 now even though I am unsure of my prospects for getting a visa appointment? A: Yes, we encourage students to [apply for their I-20/DS-2019](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/newly-admitted/before-you-arrive/get-a-us-visa/apply-for-an-i20-or-ds2019.html) as soon as possible, pay the SEVIS fee, and get documents in order to make an appointment.
