Q: Can I use Sprintax to file for my dependents? A: Yes, Sprintax is offered free of charge to the NYU community and their dependents. Please use the link from our [tax page](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/current-students/employment-and-tax/tax.html) to access the Sprintax software.
