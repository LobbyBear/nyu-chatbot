Q: Where can I find information about housing and living in New York? A: On-Campus Housing - visit the [on-campus living](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/housing-and-dining.html) website. Off-Campus Housing - [for more information.](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/housing-and-dining/off-campus-living.html#quadBox_nyurichtext) You can also find off-campus housing and roommates through the [NYU Housing Registry](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/housing-and-dining/off-campus-living/step-6.html). The <i>[International Student's Survival Guide to Living in NYC](https://one-to-world.dpdcart.com/cart/view?product_id=180050&amp;method_id=194427#/) </i>that you received once your I-20 or DS-2019 was ready that also has a listing of temporary housing in New York.
