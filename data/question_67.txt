Q: When can I enter the United States and how long can I stay? A: All F-1 and J-1 students, including those admitted to short-term programs, may enter the United States up to 30 days before the program start date on the I-20 or DS-2019. F-1 students can stay in the United States for up to 60 days after their program end date, while J-1 students can stay in the United States for up to 30 days.
