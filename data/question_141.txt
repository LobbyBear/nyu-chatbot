Q: What should I do if I did not file a tax return for the previous year(s)? A: International visitors (this includes students and scholars) and their dependents are required to file tax documents each year they were present in the United States. Sprintax can assist you in filing previous tax returns. You can use their Live Chat system when logged in to Sprintax or email hello@sprintax.com. You may also visit the IRS website for information on [Filing Past Due Tax Returns](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/filing-past-due-tax-returns).
