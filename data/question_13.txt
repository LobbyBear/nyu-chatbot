Q: What happens if my visa is denied? A: If you receive a visa denial, request a denial letter from the US Consulate so that you will understand why your visa was denied. The main reason visas are denied for the US is failure of the applicant to prove they have ties to their home country. Learn more about [visa denials](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/us-visas/visa-information-resources/visa-denials.html). OGS would like to support you in any way that we can.
