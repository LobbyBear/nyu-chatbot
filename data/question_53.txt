Q: How do I open a bank account? A: To open an account, take your passport, I-20, NYU ID Card, an [enrollment verification](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/registration-records-and-graduation/transcripts-certifications-grades.html#tripleBox_nyurichtext) (you must first [add your US address ](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/newly-admitted/when-you-arrive.html#tripleBox_nyurichtext_17)to [Albert](http://albert.nyu.edu) and register for classes before obtaining the enrollment verification) and cash or check to deposit.
