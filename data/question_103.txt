Q: What if my application is rejected or denied? A: [Contact us](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/contact-us.html) or come to [our advising hours](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/contact-us.html#walkin) immediately, along with your rejection/denial notice, so that we may help you determine whether you can resubmit your application, or have your application reconsidered.
