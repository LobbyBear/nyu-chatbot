Q: Where can I access computers and printers at NYU? A: NYU Information Technology Services (ITS) offers internet accounts to NYU students enrolled in degree or diploma programs. [Activate your NYU account](http://start.nyu.edu) with you NetID. Check out the various resources at the [NYU technology labs](https://www.nyu.edu/life/information-technology/locations-and-facilities/compare-nyu-it-locations.html) for access to computers, laser printers, CD-ROM drives, scanners and related equipment, as well as a wide selection of software.
