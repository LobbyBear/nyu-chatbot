Q: What is an I-20/DS-2019? A: The [I-20 (for F-1 students)/DS-2019 (for J-1 students)](https://www.nyu.edu/students/student-information-and-resources/student-visa-and-immigration/newly-admitted/before-you-arrive/get-a-us-visa/apply-for-an-i20-or-ds2019.html) is the document you need from NYU in order to apply for the F-1 or J-1 student visa at a consulate outside the US. You also need to present your I-20 or DS-2019 at the US border along with your F-1 or J-1 visa (except for Canadian citizens), and other supporting documents in order to be able to enter the US as an F-1 or J-1 student.
