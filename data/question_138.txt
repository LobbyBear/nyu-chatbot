Q: Can I work remotely while on STEM OPT? A: Yes, students participating in STEM OPT may work remotely as long as they continue to comply with F-1 STEM OPT requirements and continue the training's mentoring relationship as indicated on the Form I-983. For remote employment, students should indicate the employer address on the Form I-983 and the site address on the I-983 should reflect the remote location.
