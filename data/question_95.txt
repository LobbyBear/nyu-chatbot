Q: What is the difference between Curricular Practical Training (CPT) and Optional Practical Training (OPT)? A: CPT and OPT are both types of off-campus work authorization for F-1 students. However, CPT must be part of your established curriculum. Meanwhile, OPT refers to optional employment directly related to your program.
